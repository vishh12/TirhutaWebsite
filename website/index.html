Who We Are

Leading the Biogas Revolution

Tirhuta Urja Udyog Pvt Ltd is a clean energy company focused on developing, owning, and operating Compressed Bio-Gas (CBG) and organic fertilizer infrastructure across India.

We build scalable waste-to-energy assets that convert agricultural and organic waste into commercially viable, low-carbon fuels and soil-enhancing by-products.

Operating at the intersection of energy security, climate action, and rural value creation, we align closely with India's SATAT initiative.
